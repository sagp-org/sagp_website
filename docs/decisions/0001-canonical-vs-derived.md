# ADR-0001

## Canonical vs. Derived Knowledge

The SAGP Digital Platform distinguishes between canonical knowledge and
derived knowledge.

Canonical knowledge is authored and maintained by the Society.

Derived knowledge is computed deterministically from canonical knowledge for
the purpose of improving usability.

Renderers may compute derived knowledge but shall never modify, replace, or
reinterpret canonical knowledge.

This distinction is a foundational architectural principle of the platform.
